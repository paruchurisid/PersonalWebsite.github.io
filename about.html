About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Siddhartha Paruchuri, I am currently a Junior at the Ohio State University majoring in Computer Science and Engineering with a minor in Business and a specialization in Artificial Intelligence. Outside of school I have a part time job working on cars for Bridgestone Retail Operations, Firestone Complete Auto Care as a Maintence Technician.

At The Ohio State University I am involved in clubs such as HACK OHIO, AI Club, Big Data Analytics, Phi Alpha Delta, The Order of the Cardinal, OSU Mountaineers and Cooking With Communities. In HACK OHI/O I am one of the Judging Team's Leading Members and a member of the Mentorship Team and I hold a position on the Executive Board of Cooking with Communities.

In my free time I enjoy traveling most of all. I love exploring new places and talking to all different types of people. The place that I think sticks out to me most was my trip to New York City. This was one of the most significant trips that I think I have ever taken because I did it by myself and I got to meet people of all different kinds when I was there.
